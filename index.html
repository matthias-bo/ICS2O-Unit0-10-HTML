The white tiger!

Many zoos around the globe have white tigers, and these exhibits can certainly attract plenty of attention and lots of visitors.
While it does happen, breeding these tigers in zoos is frowned upon and many zookeepers elect to have the tigers spayed or neutered
to prevent breeding from occurring. White tiger facts show that in the wild, illegal poaching is all too common, as the white coats
can fetch a high price on the black market. Whether they are in captivity or in the wild, there are many programs in place that serve
to protect white tigers from illegal hunting or improper living conditions. -facts cheif from www.facts.net
